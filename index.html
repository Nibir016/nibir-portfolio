Here’s My Process

Simple, transparent, and focused on getting you results.

01

Share Your Channel & Video Idea

You send me your channel name and your video topic. I’ll also do my own research to understand your niche, audience, and what works in your space.

02

Discussion & First Sketch

We discuss the idea and I come up with a rough concept/sketch. You can also send reference thumbnails if you want something in a similar style.

03

Design & Revisions

Once we agree on the direction, I start designing the thumbnail. You can ask for revisions until you’re fully satisfied.

04

Final Delivery

You get the final, polished thumbnail — ready to upload and get clicks.
